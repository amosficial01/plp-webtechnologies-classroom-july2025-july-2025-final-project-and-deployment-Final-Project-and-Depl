Innovation Timeline

Our journey of continuous innovation in smart transportation technology.

Company Founded

2018

SmartMove was established with a vision to revolutionize urban transportation through AI-powered solutions and sustainable mobility practices.

First Smart Bus Network

2019

Launched our first AI-optimized bus network in Metro City, reducing wait times by 30% and improving route efficiency through real-time data analysis.

Mobile Platform Launch

2020

Released our integrated mobile app with multi-modal journey planning, real-time tracking, and contactless payment capabilities.

Traffic AI System

2021

Developed and deployed our intelligent traffic management system, reducing citywide congestion by 25% through adaptive signal control.

International Expansion

2022

Expanded operations to 25 cities across 3 continents, establishing partnerships with local governments and transportation authorities.

Carbon Neutral Fleet

2023

Achieved 100% carbon neutrality across our vehicle fleet through electric vehicle adoption and renewable energy integration.

Future Mobility Lab

2024

Established our research and development center focused on autonomous vehicles, urban air mobility, and next-generation transportation technologies.
